Our goal

From joining DfE, getting involved in our communities of practice, to growing skills and knowledge, we want to create the best environment for designers, anywhere in government.

The working group is made up of people from across content, interaction, and service design.

Our initiatives

Improving the onboarding experience In progress

We have:

  • spoken to recent new-starters about their experience
  • mapped out the onboarding journey
  • audited all the resources that people are sent when they join
  • started to look at what a good onboarding journey looks like
Create a set of values and principles by which we work Not started

Establish a set of values and principles which outline how we do design in DfE.

Bringing together the design professions and setting expectations for how we do design and how we work together.

Develop career pathways Not started

Understand the skills and capabilities needed to move into, or between, design professions.

How we continually develop skills and capability Not started

Training opportunities, skill sharing and developing our people.

Keeping on top of changing design methods, developing standards, and design maturity.
