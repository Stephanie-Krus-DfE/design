We listen, learn,
and grow

We're always learning, from feedback, from each other, to grow and develop our profession.

We share our work and talk about it openly

This helps everyone understand what we're doing and why. We don't work behind closed doors.

We put our
users first

We design for our users, not the loudest voice in the room.

We use human-centred
design principles

We use industry standards, government design principles, and laws of UX. Not whats trendy.

We solve the
root cause problem

Not what someone thinks is the problem - we keep asking why and how can we make this better

We fight for
accessibility and inclusivity

We shouldn't have to, but we bloody well have to.

We all work for the same organisation

There is no us and them, we are one design community.

We design for sustainability and performance

Don't get fancy, we design and make things that are efficient and work for our users.

We stick to these
values and principles

But they aren't set in stone, they can, and will, change.
